Sound familiar?

Most businesses aren't short on data or tools.
They're short on usable systems.

The issue is rarely one missing dashboard. It is usually scattered data, repeated manual work, and workflows that depend on people copying information between systems.

01

Disconnected systems

Sales, finance, customer and operations data live in different tools that don't talk to each other, so no one has a single, trustworthy view of the business.

02

Reporting that looks back, not forward

You can see what happened last month. What's missing is a reliable read on what's likely to happen next, which workflow is slowing the team down, and what action to take.

03

No time to dig into it

Teams lose hours to checking spreadsheets, rewriting updates, moving data, chasing exceptions and answering the same internal questions again and again.

My approach

Think big.
Start small.
Prove it works.

You don't need a year-long transformation programme to get value from AI and analytics. You need one well-chosen workflow or decision, a practical pilot that proves itself quickly, and a clear path to scale it safely.

01

Understand

We start with your goals, key decisions, repeated workflows and the places where current data, reporting or manual processes are slowing the business down.

02

Pilot

I design and build a small, focused solution - dashboard, forecast, automation or AI agent - aimed at one real business problem, so you see value in weeks, not quarters.

03

Prove

We review what the pilot found, what time or cost it saved, how reliable the outputs are, and whether it should be expanded.

04

Scale

Once the value is clear, I build the production-ready version: documented, reliable, monitored, and handed over so your team can run with it.
