Common problems I help solve

Most businesses aren't short on data.
They're short on clarity.

You're likely sitting on more information than you've ever had — and still making your biggest calls on gut feel. Here's where that usually shows up.

01

Disconnected systems

Sales, finance and operations data live in different tools that don't talk to each other, so no one has a single, trustworthy view of the business.

02

Reporting that looks back, not forward

You can see what happened last month. What's missing is a reliable read on what's about to happen — and what to do about it.

03

No time to dig into it

The insight is probably in there somewhere. But nobody on the team has the hours, or the analytical background, to go and find it.
